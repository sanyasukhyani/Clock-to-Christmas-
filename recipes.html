Fruitcake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed dried fruit
  • 1 cup walnuts, chopped
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup dark rum

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 325Â°F (160Â°C). Grease and line a baking pan.
  2. Mix the flour, baking powder, and walnuts together in a bowl.
  3. Cream butter and sugar in a separate bowl, then add eggs and rum.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients, then fold in dried fruit.
  5. Pour into the pan and bake for 1.5 hours. Let cool before serving.

Sugar Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/2 cups butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar (for rolling)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F (175Â°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Mix flour and baking soda in a bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add egg and vanilla, and beat until fluffy.
  4. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients until dough forms.
  5. Roll dough into balls, roll in sugar, and place on baking sheets.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes until edges are golden. Let cool before serving.

Chocolate Truffles

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder (for rolling)
  • Chopped nuts (optional)

Steps:

  1. Heat heavy cream in a saucepan until it just starts to simmer.
  2. Pour hot cream over the chopped chocolate and stir until smooth.
  3. Mix in vanilla extract and let cool to room temperature. Chill in the fridge for 2 hours.
  4. Once firm, scoop and roll into balls. Roll in cocoa powder or chopped nuts.
  5. Chill again for 30 minutes before serving.

Gingerbread

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda

  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup molasses

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F (175Â°C). Grease baking sheets.
  2. Whisk together flour, baking soda, and spices in a bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat together butter and brown sugar. Add egg and molasses.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ingredients, mixing until dough forms.
  5. Roll out dough, cut into shapes, and place on baking sheets.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes. Let cool before serving or decorating.
